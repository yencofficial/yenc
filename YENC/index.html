About Us

Who We Are

The Youth Empowerment Network Calauag (YENC) is a youth-led, non-profit organization dedicated to developing responsible, empowered, and community-driven young leaders across Calauag, Quezon.

Founded with the vision of uniting young people for meaningful change, YENC provides a platform for leadership training, civic engagement, community outreach, and personal growth. We create safe spaces where youth can discover their potential and take action on issues that matter to them.

Through diverse programs and community partnerships, YENC works to strengthen youth participation in local development — because we believe the youth of Calauag are not just the future, they are the present.

Our Foundation

Mission & Vision

The principles and purpose that guide every action YENC takes in the community.

Our Mission

To empower the youth of Calauag by providing opportunities for leadership development, community involvement, and personal growth — through programs that inspire service, creativity, and a deep sense of responsibility.

Our Vision

A united and empowered youth of Calauag who are proactive leaders, socially responsible citizens, and catalysts of positive change — making their community, province, and nation a better place for all.

Core Values

Leadership — Developing confident and responsible young leaders
Service — Commitment to helping the community selflessly
Integrity — Upholding honesty and strong moral principles
Unity — Building teamwork and collaboration among youth
Empowerment — Encouraging self-growth and building confidence
Creativity — Promoting innovation and expression among young people

Our Community

Proudly rooted in Calauag, Quezon, every program YENC runs is designed around the specific needs, aspirations, and challenges of local youth. We work hand-in-hand with community stakeholders, schools, and local government every step of the way.

Our Goals

What We Strive For

The measurable objectives that drive YENC's programs and initiatives forward.

01
To provide leadership training and youth development programs that equip young people with the skills to lead effectively in their communities.
02
To promote active participation in community service and outreach activities that create tangible, positive change in Calauag.
03
To develop essential life skills including communication, critical thinking, teamwork, and problem-solving among young members.
04
To encourage youth involvement in social, civic, and environmental issues that affect their lives and the lives of those around them.
05
To create meaningful collaboration opportunities between different youth sectors, schools, organizations, and community stakeholders.
What We Do

Programs & Activities

YENC runs diverse programs designed to nurture youth leadership, community spirit, and meaningful personal growth.

Leadership Training Workshops
Hands-on seminars that equip young people with leadership skills — from public speaking and strategic thinking to team management and decision-making.
Community Outreach Programs
Volunteer initiatives that bring YENC members into the community to address local needs through direct service, care, and meaningful action.
Youth Forums & Assemblies
Open platforms where youth voices are heard, ideas are exchanged, and young leaders engage in conversations about issues that matter most to them.
Environmental Campaigns
Grassroots initiatives including clean-up drives, tree planting activities, and community awareness campaigns for sustainable and responsible living.
Skills Development Seminars
Practical workshops covering communication, digital literacy, financial awareness, entrepreneurship, and other life skills critical for today's youth.
Sports & Cultural Events
Tournaments, cultural showcases, and recreational events that foster teamwork, cultural pride, physical wellness, and community spirit.
Advocacy Campaigns
Targeted campaigns raising awareness on mental health, access to education, gender equality, and other pressing social issues affecting youth.
